LinkedIn Unsubscribed

If you are tired of LinkedIn emails, you are only 21 options away from unsubscribing them. Why they have this disrespectful UX is beyond my understanding. (And yes, A/B tests can lie.)

Well, let's end this madness.

How to use the bookmarklet

  1. Drag the following link onto your bookmark bar (usually it is right underneath your address bar)
  2. Go to LinkedIn's email settings page
  3. Click on the bookmark you just made
  4. Now do more meaningful work than dealing with spamming emails
